Invited Speaker

We are pleased to announce the keynote speaker for the Beyond a Concrete Workshop at ICMI 2026:

Gabriella Vigliocco
UCL – Language and Cognition Lab, UK
Lab Website

Keynote Talk

Title: Grounding Abstract Concepts in Multimodal Interaction
Abstract: Abstract concepts pose a fundamental challenge for multimodal interactive systems, as they cannot be grounded in single perceptual features or fixed motor patterns; instead, they require abstraction across perception, action, language, and affect. In this talk, Gabriella Vigliocco will discuss computational and interactional mechanisms for grounding abstract concepts beyond concrete sensory inputs, highlighting work from multimodal representation learning, embodied and developmental models, and hybrid cognitive architectures. The talk will also emphasize evaluation strategies and open challenges in the field.
